# Who we are?
<p align="justify">
<ul>
<li><b>Brief Description</b></li>
Pet on Click is an app that aims to promote the adoption of pets. The app connects people who offer their pets for adoption and people who want to adopt, as the app allows you to sort the results by region, type, age, and gender of the pet. The app provides pet supplies and immediate consultation service with a veterinarian.


<li><b>Objectives</b></li>
<ol type="1">
<li>Promote a culture of pet adoption.</li>
<li>Provide care for pets and support the public interest.</li>
<li>Provide the best pet food and supplies products, and deliver them from the warehouse to the customer's address.</li>
<li>Getting medical consultation with a vet at any time.</li>
</ol>

<li><b>The market need</b></li>
<ul>
<li><b>Our Customers</b></li>
Our customers are individuals between the ages of 21-40, with average income or above, living in Saudi Arabia.
<li><b>Needs will my product will satisfy</b></li>
<ol type="1">
<li>The desire to offer pets for adoption for a period of time for those who are willing.</li>
<li>The desire to adopt a pet without having to buy another pet.</li>
<li>The desire to purchase pet supplies online with more available options and high quality.</li>
<li>The desire to get an immediate consultation for a pet that the customer owns.</li>
</ol>
<li><b>Potential Clients</b></li>
The number of potential clients who live in the area where we want to operate ranges from 3,000 to 5,000. Potential customers currently adopt through shelters, associations, or through accounts on social media such as Twitter, they buy pet supplies from physical stores or online stores. Pet owners seek medical advice from a local vet or an online vet. Potential customers are willing to pay affordable prices for our products, the product prices range from a minimum of 5 SR to a maximum of 250 SR.
</ul>

# Functional & Non-fuctional Requirements
<p align="justify">
<ul>
<li><b>Functional Requirements</b></li>

We categorize our functional requirements according to the type of the users. The system will be used by 4 actors: Adopter, Buyer, Adoption Presenter, and Guest.
<ul>
<li><b>Adopter & Buyer</b></li>
<ol type="1">
<li>They should be able to sign up.</li>
<li>They should be able to sign in using a username, and password.</li>
<li>They should be able to view a list of pets, and view each animal's name, type, gender, age, location, and contact data of the presenter such as email, and phone number.</li>
<li>They should be able to view the list of products, view the details of each product, and purchase the product.</li>
<li>They should be able to set up an instant consultation session and send text messages.</li>
<li>They should be able to edit thier personal profile.</li>
</ol>

<li><b>Adoption Presenter</b></li>
<ol type="1">
<li>He should be able to sign up.</li>
<li>He should be able to sign in using a username, and password.</li>
<li>He should be able to view a list of pets, and view each animal's name, type, gender, age, location, and contact data of the presenter such as email, and phone number.</li>
<li>He should be able to view the list of products, view the details of each product, and purchase the product.</li>
<li>He should be able to set up an instant consultation session and send text messages.</li>
<li>He should be able to create an adoption offer, fill in the required fields and upload a pet picture.</li>
<li>He should be able to edit his personal profile.</li>
</ol>

<li><b>Guest</b></li>
<ol type="1">
<li>He should be able to view a list of pets, and view each animal's name, type, gender, age, and location.</li>
<li>He should be able to view the list of products, and view the details of each product.</li>
</ol>
</ul>
